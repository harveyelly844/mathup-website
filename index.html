What we do

A few ways to learn, one focus: A-Level Math & Physics.

Holiday tutoring

Focused tutoring during school holidays in A-Level Mathematics and Physics — and we can take on other subjects too, depending on what a student needs.

Free Zoom seminars

Scheduled sessions on Zoom that walk through topics, past-paper techniques, and the questions students actually struggle with. Always free to join.

In-person tutoring

Where it suits a student better, we offer in-person tutoring too — this isn't an online-only operation.

YouTube lesson library

Recorded lessons you can revisit anytime — for revision the night before a test, or to go back over a topic a second time at your own pace.

Why Math-Up

Math doesn't have to be the subject you dread.

  • Content built specifically around what A-Level students in Uganda are actually examined on.
  • No paywalls on the basics — Zoom seminars and YouTube lessons are free to join and watch.
  • Explanations from people who recently sat the same papers, not just textbook recitals.
  • A growing, student-run initiative — not a one-off tutoring gig.

"Get your Math up!" isn't just a tagline — it's the whole point. Whatever level you're starting from, the goal is to move it up.

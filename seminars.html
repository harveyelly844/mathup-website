How it works

Joining a seminar is simple.

1. Get notified

Join our WhatsApp group or follow our LinkedIn page — seminar dates, times, and topics are announced there first.

2. Join on Zoom

Sessions run live on Zoom — no fee, no application. Just join with the link shared ahead of the seminar.

3. Revisit anytime

Most seminars are uploaded to YouTube afterward, so you can rewatch a topic before a test or exam.

What we cover

Topics drawn straight from the A-Level Mathematics and Physics syllabuses.

Seminars focus on the areas that tend to decide exam performance — pure mathematics techniques, statistics, mechanics, and physics concepts, worked through with real past-paper style questions rather than abstract theory alone.

  • Step-by-step walkthroughs of past-paper questions
  • Common mistakes and how examiners actually award marks
  • Topic-by-topic revision sessions ahead of major exams
  • Time for students to ask questions live
